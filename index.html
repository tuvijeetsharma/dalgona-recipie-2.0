☕Dalgona Coffee Recipie☕

This yummy South Korean coffee treat that's all over the internet only requires four ingredients to make - all of which you probably have in the cupboard – you feel like you’ve won the jackpot, right?

Ingredients

  • Instant Coffee
  • Sugar
  • Hot Water
  • Milk

Dalgona Making Process

Step 1: Combine the instant coffee, sugar, and hot water:
1.Add 2 tablespoons (tbsp) of instant coffee then Add 2 tablespoons (tbsp) of sugar and then Add 2 tablespoons (tbsp) of hot water

Step 2: Whisk, whisk, whisk until the coffee is light and fluffy!
2. Using an electric hand-held mixer, whip the coffee mixture until it is light brown, fluffy, and holds stiff peaks when the whisk is removed.

Step 3: Serve with Milk:
3. Use a spoon to garnish a glass of hot or cold milk with the coffee cream. Serve with a straw, and stir, stir, stir to enjoy!
